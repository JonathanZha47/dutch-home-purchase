## Points that may lower fair price

### 1. Small usable indoor area (~50.5 m²) — high impact

The NEN 2580 meetcertificaat (June 2026) measures only **50.5 m² living + 4.7 m² OIR**. That is much smaller than other Hoofddorp options in the shortlist (e.g. Nieuweweg ~70 m²).

**Price impact:** If Funda/listing advertises a larger m² figure, recalculate €/m² before comparing or bidding.

### 2. Seller disclosed moisture history — high impact until clarified

On the signed **Vragenlijst Deel B**, the seller checked **“ja”** to past/current moisture on floors/walls/ceilings, but **left location and details blank**.

**Price impact:** Treat as a real defect risk until inspected. Ask for location, cause, repairs, and photos; consider a **bouwkundige keuring** or moisture clause in the purchase agreement.

### 3. Future VvE capital expenses — medium impact

MJOP (Aug 2021, **needs updating**) shows large building-wide costs:

| Year | Major MJOP costs (whole building) | Rough per-unit share (~30 units) |
|------|-------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| **2024** | ~€84k (already largely executed: lift repair ~€10k, exterior painting ~€149k cycle) | Already absorbed |
| **2030** | ~€**134k** (roof layer, paving, drainage, etc.) | ~€**4,500**/unit if fully passed through |
| **2036** | ~€**118k** (lighting, intercom, etc.) | ~€**3,900**/unit |
| **2048** | Full roof replacement ~€41k+ | Later cycle |

**Reserve fund (Dec 2025):** **€102,107** — healthy today (~€3,400/unit average), and the VvE adds ~€17,750/year to reserves.

**But:** April 2026 minutes say *“no major maintenance planned until 2030”* while the MJOP clearly peaks in **2030**. That mismatch is a negotiation point.

**Also voted (April 2026):**

- Parking bollards: up to **€20,000** (from reserve)
- Camera system: up to **€3,000** (from reserve)

**Price impact:** Not an immediate dealbreaker, but budget **€3k–5k** future herstellingsbijdrage risk in the 2030 window, or bid lower to compensate.

### 4. Rising VvE costs — medium impact

- **2026** budget: **€59,860** (building total)
- **2027:** approved **+3.5%** on VvE bijdrage

For 854V, expect monthly VvE to rise from **€147.32** toward ~**€152–153** in 2027.

### 5. Seller disclosure quality — medium impact

Seller lived there ~**1.5 years** and left many answers as *“don’t know”* or blank, including:

- VvE reserve amount, MJOP, monthly VvE fee (section 16)
- Build year, energy label (“I think C?” — **conflicts with official Label A**)

**Price impact:** Rely on documents + your own inspection, not verbal assurances.

### 6. VvE payment arrears — low/medium

2025 jaarverslag shows **€6,897** debiteuren “achterstand”. Not catastrophic for a 30-unit VvE, but worth confirming it’s not chronic.

| Factor | Direction |
